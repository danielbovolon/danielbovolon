DANIEL BOVOLON IS AN ITALIAN SOUND DESIGNER FROM BOLZANO/BOZEN, BASED IN BERLIN.

Daniel has worked on projects spanning feature films, commercials, and exploratory sound projects. His practice is grounded in attentive listening and a sensitivity to space, vibration and rhythm, making musical structures emerge from unexpected spaces.

All sound works presented on this site are conceived and produced by Daniel.
